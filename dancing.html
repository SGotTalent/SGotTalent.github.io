Dancing Group Competition

REQUIRES SCHOOL REGISTRATION

Freestyle dance competition with an aim to encourage students to participate in activities beyond learning. It is a platform to promote teamwork, boost confidence levels and inspire students to connect culturally through the art of dance .

Our vision is to create an exciting competition bringing together children of all ages and abilities where the emphasis is on having a good time.

Each school can enter up to 5 entries (in each Key Stage). Whatever your preferred style of dancing, whatever your level of experience, we want you to be involved. A bit of imagination, dedication and teamwork is all that is required.

REQUIRES SCHOOL REGISTRATION

Details :
JUNIOR & MIDDLE & HIGH SCHOOL COMPETITION

  • Junior : 7 to 10 years old (on 31 September 2022)
  • Middle : 11 to 13 years old (on 31 September 2022)
  • Senior : 14 to 18 years old (on 31 September 2022)

Rules :
  • Entry is FREE with ONLINE REGISTRATION. The competition rules are designed to be as easy and as fair as possible providing consistent criteria for our judges and maximum fun for our teams
  • Entry is open to all International Schools from Junior, Middle and High School.
  • Maximun 5 teams / school with no teams limit
  • Generally we advise the group to be no larger than 30 pupils
  • Your dance should be no longer than 3m30s.
  • Any style of dance is permitted
The Judging Criteria Up to 100 marks to be awarded for each performance. Choreography: Creativity (10 points) Complexity (10 points) Formations/Transitions (10 points) Execution: Synchronisation/Timing (10 points) Spacing/Dynamics (10 points) Technique: Placing/Control (10 points) Strength/Fluidity (10 points) Technique/Connectivity (10 points) Overall: Eye Contact/Enjoyability (10 points) Crowd Appeal (10 points)
Schedule

Registration Opens 1st October 2022
Submission Deadline 15th November 2022
Round 1 Judging 15th January 2023
Round 2 Judging 15th February 2023
Finals & Winner Announcement 17th March 2023
ROUND 1: The contest begins with a preliminary round, in which students dance pre-recorded song and uploaded whilst registration. Schools are responsible for their video including their music with submission. The school sends all materials under the registration number received in the registration labeled by stage Name. Ex: Registration No_Stage_ Team no_Dance Name Ex: 1024_Junior_ Team 1_HIPO Please submit your video in high-quality MP4 format for better results. We also encourage all competitors to submit videos that are filmed HORIZONTAL, if you are recording from your phone ROUND 2: Life performance on stage. (OPERA HOUSE). ROUND 3: Finalist (TOP 5) will be life performance on stage in front of parents and audience in a Big festival 17th MARCH 2023.

WINNER & PRICES

The Final match will be 17th of March 2023. Cairo Hockey Stadium
