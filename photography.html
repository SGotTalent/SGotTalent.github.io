Photography Competition

Photography is a great medium as you can approach it from many angles from the super-technical to the purely artistic, and it can inspire groups that struggle with other art forms. Most people have access to a phone with a camera, and that’s all you need to take part. If your child is constantly looking for the best angle, lighting and composition for his/her shots, you shouldn't let their talent be wasted

We invites you to submit a photograph that best conveys what makes you happy, along with a "Happiness is …" caption. (ex: "Happiness is watching Children laugh, Happiness is receiving a dog hug“.. etc..)

Details :
MIDDLE & Senior COMPETITION

  • Middle : 11 to 13 years old (on 31 September 2022)
  • Senior : 14 to 18 years old (on 31 September 2022)

Rules :
  • Entry is open to all International Schools from Junior, Middle and High School.
  • Each contestant allowed to upload maximun 3 photos / category & pictures must have a happiness description. Entries must have been taken between 1 OCT. 2022 and 31 DEC. 2022
  • Images should be submitted in PDF, PNG or JPEG format at high-resolution. No RAW images or TIFF images will be accepted. File size should not exceed 10-megapixels.
  • The photo must be at least 1,600 pixels wide or tall (if horizontal or vertical image respectively)
  • Images should also be identified and dated, and include camera-recorded metadata.
  • Contestants are not allowed to digitally alter, remove or change objects in a photograph or add special effect enhancements to photo submissions.
  • Note ** “Overall Color Enhancement,” Increased Contrast, Overall Lightening or Darkening, and Image Cropping are the only acceptable enhancements for the above categories.
